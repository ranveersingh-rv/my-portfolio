Hi, I'm Ranveer Singh — an aspiring fashion and lifestyle model focused on building a powerful, industry-level portfolio that reflects confidence, personality, versatility, and strong camera presence.

I'm actively growing my career in modeling and continuously working on improving my fashion sense, posing, expressions, physique, and overall visual identity.

Recently, I achieved 3rd place in Parul University Season 3 – PU's Rising Star, which helped me gain more exposure, confidence, and experience in front of audiences and judges. I was also a finalist in the Mr. and Miss DOOM competition, where I successfully cleared multiple rounds and reached the final stage. I was also featured in a magazine shoutout.

These experiences strengthened my stage presence, confidence, grooming, and modeling abilities.
